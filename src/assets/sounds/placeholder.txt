This directory is for game sounds.

Required sound files:
1. title_bgm.mp3 - Title screen background music
2. map_bgm.mp3 - Map screen background music
3. battle_bgm.mp3 - Battle background music
4. victory_bgm.mp3 - Victory music
5. defeat_bgm.mp3 - Defeat music
6. ending_bgm.mp3 - Ending music
7. button_click.wav - Button click sound effect
8. attack.wav - Attack sound effect
9. damage.wav - Damage sound effect
10. item_use.wav - Item use sound effect
11. level_up.wav - Level up sound effect

You can create these sound files using audio tools or find suitable free-to-use sound effects.
